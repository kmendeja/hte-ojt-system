ABOUT THE SYSTEM

On-the-Job Training (OJT) plays a significant role in preparing students for the professional environment by allowing them to gain hands-on experience. This web-based application is designed to enhance the efficiency and transparency of the On-the-Job Training (OJT) tracking process through the digitization of logs and reports, facilitating real-time monitoring of trainee activities, and streamlining the evaluation of student performance. Furthermore, the system incorporates a comprehensive profiling feature, enabling the creation and maintenance of detailed trainee records encompassing personal information, and training progress. This unified platform aims to provide a reliable and efficient environment wherein both students and coordinators can effectively manage their respective responsibilities within the OJT program.
